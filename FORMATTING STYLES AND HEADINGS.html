FORMATTING STYLES AND HEADINGS

BOLD TAG

Bold tag is used to bold any letter or alphabets in a paragraph.

ITALICS TAG

Italic tag helps to italic any alphabet or letter in a paragraph.

UNDERLINE TAG

Underline tag helps to underline any alphabet or letter in a paragraph.

STRIKE TAG

Strike tag helps to cut or strike any alphabet or letter in a paragraph.

SUBSCIPT TAG

Subscript tag helps to write anything under a letter or alphabet in a paragraph.

SUPSCIPT TAG

Supscript tag helps to write anything above a letter or alphabet in a paragraph.
